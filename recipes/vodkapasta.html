Vodka Pasta

Description

This is a great italian recipe. Pasta with a twist. My girlfriend makes it very good.

Ingredients

  • 1/4th cup olive oil
  • 1 small clove garlic (diced)
  • 1/4th green onioin
  • 1/4th cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp vodka
  • 1/2 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup parmasean
  • salt
  • pepper
  • pinch of sugar
  • pasta

Directions

    1) Heat olive oil, garlic & onion in saucepan
    2) Add tomato paste
    3) Add heavy cream & vodka
    4) Add red pepper flakes and a pinch of vinegar
    5) Add salt & pepper
    6) Boil pasta (save water)
    7) Add butter, parmesean, and some pasta water
    8) Combine sauce and drained pasta
    9) Top with parmesean & basil garnish
    10) Enjoy :)
